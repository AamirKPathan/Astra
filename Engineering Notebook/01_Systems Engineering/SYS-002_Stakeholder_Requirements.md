# 1. Purpose

This document defines the stakeholder requirements for the ASTRA Autonomous Flight System (AAFS).

Stakeholder requirements describe the needs, expectations, and operational objectives of individuals and groups that interact with or are affected by the system. These requirements form the basis for the Functional Requirements and System Requirements that follow.

# 3. Stakeholder Needs

The ASTRA system should provide:

- Safe and reliable operation.
- Autonomous flight capabilities.
- Accurate telemetry.
- Reliable data logging.
- Modular hardware and software.
- Expandable system architecture.
- Clear engineering documentation.
- Simple maintenance and testing procedures.
- Reliable recovery support.
- Effective post-flight analysis.

---

# 4. Stakeholder Requirements

### SHR-001

The system shall operate safely throughout all phases of the mission.

---

### SHR-002

The system shall minimize required operator interaction during nominal flight.

---

### SHR-003

The system shall provide real-time telemetry to the Ground Control Station.

---

### SHR-004

The system shall provide sufficient mission data for engineering analysis.

---

### SHR-005

The system shall support authorized remote operator intervention during emergency situations.

---

### SHR-006

The system shall support safe recovery operations.

---

### SHR-007

The system shall maintain a modular architecture that permits future subsystem expansion.

---

### SHR-008

The system shall provide comprehensive engineering documentation throughout development.

---

### SHR-009

The system shall support simulation prior to hardware implementation whenever practical.

---

### SHR-010

The system shall support repeatable testing and verification procedures.

---

### SHR-011

The system shall provide clear diagnostic information for troubleshooting.

---

### SHR-012

The system shall maintain configuration traceability throughout the project lifecycle.

---

### SHR-013

The system shall maintain compatibility with the ASTRA Ground Control Station.

---

### SHR-014

The system shall preserve flight data following mission completion.

---

### SHR-015

The system shall support continuous engineering improvement through iterative development.

---

# 5. Stakeholder Priorities

The relative priority of stakeholder needs is as follows:

1. Safety
2. Reliability
3. Mission Success
4. System Integrity
5. Data Collection
6. Modularity
7. Expandability
8. Maintainability
9. Ease of Operation
10. Performance

---

# 6. Assumptions

The following assumptions apply:

- Operators are trained in system operation.
- Ground support equipment is available.
- Pre-flight procedures have been completed.
- Communication systems are operational.
- Engineering documentation is maintained throughout development.

---

# 7. Traceability

Each stakeholder requirement shall be allocated to one or more Functional Requirements (SYS-003).

Compliance with stakeholder requirements shall be verified through inspection, analysis, demonstration, or testing as defined in the Verification Matrix.
