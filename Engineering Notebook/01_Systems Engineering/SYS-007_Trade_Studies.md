# 1. Purpose

This document records engineering trade studies performed throughout the development of the ASTRA Autonomous Flight System (AAFS).

Trade studies provide a structured process for evaluating multiple engineering solutions and selecting the option that best satisfies project requirements, constraints, and objectives.

Every major engineering decision should be supported by a documented trade study whenever practical.

---

# 2. Trade Study Process

Each trade study shall follow the process below:

1. Define the engineering problem.
2. Identify candidate solutions.
3. Establish evaluation criteria.
4. Evaluate each candidate.
5. Compare results.
6. Select the preferred solution.
7. Document the rationale.
8. Record the final decision.

---

# 3. Evaluation Criteria

Typical evaluation criteria include:

- Performance
- Reliability
- Mass
- Power Consumption
- Cost
- Availability
- Ease of Integration
- Expandability
- Maintainability
- Software Support
- Community Support
- Risk
- Complexity

Not every criterion applies to every trade study.

# 5. Trade Study Template

Each engineering trade study shall include the following sections:

## Engineering Problem

Describe the engineering problem to be solved.

---

## Candidate Solutions

Identify all reasonable candidate solutions.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Define the criteria used to evaluate each solution.

---

## Comparison

Compare each candidate against the evaluation criteria.

---

## Decision

Identify the selected solution.

---

## Justification

Provide engineering rationale supporting the selected solution.

---

## Impact

Document the impact of the decision on:

- Hardware
- Software
- Cost
- Schedule
- Performance
- Risk

---

# 6. Decision Authority

Trade study outcomes shall be approved by the Project Lead before implementation.

Approved decisions shall become part of the project baseline unless superseded by a future trade study.

---

# 7. Traceability

Each completed trade study shall reference:

- Relevant Requirements
- System Architecture
- Risk Assessments
- Verification Activities

Any resulting design changes shall be reflected in the appropriate engineering documentation.
