# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone participating in the ASTRA Autonomous Flight System project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive community include:

* Being respectful and constructive.
* Providing helpful technical feedback.
* Welcoming new contributors.
* Documenting engineering decisions clearly.
* Focusing on evidence, testing, and technical merit.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Personal attacks or harassment.
* Discrimination of any kind.
* Trolling or inflammatory comments.
* Deliberately misleading contributors.
* Sharing unsafe engineering advice as verified fact.

## Engineering Culture

ASTRA values:

* Safety-first engineering.
* Honest technical discussion.
* Thorough documentation.
* Reproducible testing.
* Continuous learning.
* Professional collaboration.

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open a private issue or contact the project maintainer.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct to maintain a productive and respectful environment.
